Before we work together...

I am positive that at some point in your life you have been wrong. However, I don't want you to be wrong about us working together. So, let me tell you a few things about working with me.

1. I love going fast

I love getting projects done quickly. My philosophy is that projects should be released to users as quickly as possible so that we can know if a project is working or not. If you share that belief and want to build something that helps people quickly, I'm your guy.

2. I do real UX

Real UX means that I talk to users. If you are not talking to users, you are not doing UX. UX requires a designer to build a basic prototype and then test it with real people. I am not afraid of talking to strangers and learning what they want. In fact, I love it! Once we have an idea, we spend time talking about how we can make it beautiful. But not until we talk to users and learn if they will use it.

3. I sing around 300% more than the average human

4. I am extremly social

I thrive working in an enviornment that allows me to meet new people, understand their ideas, and learn from them. If your job requires me to work from home most of the time in isolation, the work I produce will be far below my potential. Plus I'll go crazy.

If you are still stoked to work, I cannot wait to chat! If not, I'm glad we cut this off before we both made each other miserable.
